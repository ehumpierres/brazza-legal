## Pro Tips

1. **Lead with the kid** — Get the kid excited, parent follows
2. **Say "free" early** — Removes hesitation
3. **Hand flyer to parent** — They control the phone
4. **"Just scan"** — Makes it feel easy/instant
5. **Don't block** — Walk alongside, don't stop them

## FAQs (What People Will Ask)

**How does it work?**
> Download the app, pick a guide character, and follow the clues to find real art pieces in the museum. It's like a treasure hunt — the app gives hints and the kids search for the actual objects.

**Is it free?**
> Yes, completely free. No hidden costs.

**Can I use it with both kids?**
> Yes! The whole family can play together on one phone. The kids take turns finding pieces.

**Does the kid have to handle the phone?**
> Nope — you hold the phone, they do the hunting. You read the clues, they find the treasure.

**Is there a lot of reading?**
> There's some, but it's designed for you to read aloud to the kids. The clues are short and fun.

**Do they (or I) need to know art?**
> Not at all! The app teaches you as you go. It's about the adventure, not art history.
