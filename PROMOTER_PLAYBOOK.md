## 📱 What is Brazza?

**Brazza** is a free iPhone app that turns museum visits into scavenger hunts for families.

**How it works:**
1. Parent downloads the app (free, iPhone only)
2. Kid picks a character guide (Professor Whiskers, Captain Chrono, etc.)
3. App gives clues to find real art pieces in the museum
4. Family follows the trail together
5. At the end, they discover a "hidden treasure"

**Think of it as:** Pokémon Go meets museum education. Kids lead the adventure, parents follow.

**Key points:**
- ✅ Completely FREE
- ✅ Works at The Met right now
- ✅ Takes 30-60 minutes
- ✅ Available in English AND Spanish
- ✅ iPhone only (no Android yet)

## ✅ Pro Tips

1. **Lead with the kid** — Get the kid excited, parent follows
2. **Say "free" early** — Removes hesitation immediately
3. **Hand flyer to parent** — They control the phone
4. **"Just scan"** — Makes it feel easy and instant
5. **Don't block** — Walk alongside, don't stop people abruptly
6. **Smile and be warm** — You're giving them something fun, not selling
7. **Stay near the entrance** — Catch people going IN, not out
8. **Work as a team** — If 2 promoters, cover different sides of the steps

## ❓ FAQs (What People Will Ask)

**"How does it work?"**
> Download the app, pick a guide character, and follow the clues to find real art pieces in the museum. It's like a treasure hunt — the app gives hints and the kids search for the actual objects.

**"Is it free?"**
> Yes, completely free. No hidden costs, no subscription.

**"Can I use it with both my kids?"**
> Yes! The whole family can play together on one phone. The kids take turns finding pieces.

**"Does my kid have to handle the phone?"**
> Nope — you hold the phone, they do the hunting. You read the clues, they find the treasure.

**"Is there a lot of reading?"**
> There's some, but it's designed for you to read aloud to the kids. The clues are short and fun.

**"Do we need to know about art?"**
> Not at all! The app teaches you as you go. It's about the adventure, not art history.

**"How long does it take?"**
> About 30-60 minutes depending on which hunt you pick. You can do a shorter or longer version.

**"Does it work on Android?"**
> Not yet — iPhone only for now. Android coming soon!

**"Is it in Spanish?"**
> Yes! The app is available in both English and Spanish.

**"Do we need WiFi inside?"**
> The app works best with cell service or WiFi, but The Met has WiFi available.

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

- Don't be pushy or aggressive
- Don't block the sidewalk or stairs
- Don't argue if someone says no — just smile and move on
- Don't hand flyers to people leaving (they're done for the day)
- Don't stand in one spot — move around and stay active
